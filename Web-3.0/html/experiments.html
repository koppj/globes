Pre-defined Experiment Files

The most important ingredient of GLoBES are adequate experiment descriptions. The experiments posted here may serve as examples in using AEDL, but they may also serve as starting point for modifications. All pre-defined AEDL files contain in their header the references to their origin. The GLoBES Team would be happy to include your glb-files into this list. Please mail to globes@mpi-hd.mpg.de
